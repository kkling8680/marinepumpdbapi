Account Deletion - MarinePumpDB

MarinePumpDB respects your privacy and gives you full control over your data. You can permanently delete your account and associated data directly from within the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the MarinePumpDB app
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Go to Profile
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion request
Account deletion is performed immediately after confirmation inside the app.

Data that is deleted

  • Email address
  • Authentication credentials
  • User profile information
  • All user-generated content

Data retention

All personal data is permanently deleted once the account deletion is confirmed. No personal data is retained after deletion.

Need help?

If you experience any issues deleting your account, please contact us at support@marinepumpdb.com
